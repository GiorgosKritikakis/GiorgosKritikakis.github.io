Giorgos Kritikakis

Software Engineer | Data Analyst

Giorgos Kritikakis holds a Bachelor's (2020 University of Crete) and a Master's (2022 University of Crete) degree in Computer Science. His recent work (Analysis and Visualization of Hierarchical Graphs) includes Path/Chain decomposition techniques, Transitive closure solutions, and a general-purpose hierarchical graph drawing framework called Path-Based Framework. Early results of this framework were presented as a poster in gd2020 . A 5-minutes video tutorial of Path Based Framework: video .

Giorgos Kritikakis

Software Developer

Giorgos Kritikakis holds a graduate degree in Computer Science (2019 University of Crete). He is currently a postgraduate student at the University of Crete in the final stages of the degree. The field of interest is Algorithms and Systems Analysis. More precisely, he focuses on graph theory and develops the Path-Based Hierarchical Drawing Framework(PBF). Early results were presented as posters in gd2020. (more details link). On this link, you can find a 5-minutes video tutorial of PBF. Except PBF Giorgos will soon present innovative algorithms that address problems like path/channel decomposition, graph hierarchies, indexing schemes, transitive closure, and all the derivatives.
